`about` - Displays info about the bot.
`developer` - Shows info about the developer of the bot.
`discord` - Provides a link to ium's official discord server.
`github` - Provides a link to ium's github.
`ping` - Displays ium's ping.
`server` - Displays information and stats on your server.
`stats` - Displays stats about ium.
`user` - Displays information and stats about the user you mention.
